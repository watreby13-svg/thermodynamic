Care instructions

  • Compartments and lid: top-rack dishwasher safe
  • Outer shell: hand-wash with damp cloth
  • Silicone gaskets: remove and rinse separately
  • Avoid abrasive cleaners or scouring pads
  • Allow all parts to dry fully before storing
  • Do not microwave the assembled box
