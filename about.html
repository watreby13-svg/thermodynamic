Our story

Engineered for everyday lunches

Thermodynamic started with a simple observation: lunchboxes force you to choose between hot or cold. We figured there had to be a better way.

?

"Why can't hot and cold share the same box?"

That question sparked a year of design, prototyping, and testing.

From frustration to solution

We noticed that existing lunchboxes treat all food the same — one temperature fits all. But real lunches aren't like that. Kids want hot pasta and cold fruit. Commuters want leftover curry and fresh salad. Outdoor lovers want hot pies and cold drinks.

So we designed a lunchbox with independent compartment insulation — each section thermally separated from the others. Hot stays hot. Cold stays cold. Simple.

NZ-style lunches,
made practical

New Zealanders love bento-style eating — a main, a side, and something sweet. But traditional lunchboxes don't handle the mix of temperatures that Kiwi lunches demand. We built Thermodynamic to match the way we actually eat: varied, practical, and no-nonsense.
